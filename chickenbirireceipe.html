CHICKEN BIRIYANI RECEIPE

INGRIDENTS REQUIRED

*CHICKEN

*PLAIN YOGURT/CURD

*GINGER GARLIC PASTE

*JUICE OF LEMON

*TURMERIC POWDER

*SALT

*CURRY POWDER

*BIRIYANI MASALA POWDER

*BLACK PEPPER POWDER,RED CHILLI POWDER

*GREEN CHILLIES,CORIANDER LEAVES

*MINT LEAVES,BASMATI RICE

*VEGATABLE OIL

*CINAMON,ONIONS

*CLOVES

PROCEDURE OF THE RECEIPE

At first soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes then slice the onions finely and make a smooth paste of ginger garlic paste and keep it aside.Now take the chicken pecies and wash it in a running water.Now heat oil in a kadai and fry onion until it turns golden.Remove few fried onions in a plate for later use.Then add ginger-garlic paste and mix well. Cook on low flame until raw smell goes away.Now add salt, red chili powder and biryani masala. Mix well and cook for 2 mins.Now add chicken pieces and mix well with the masala.Then add curd and mix well. Cook on low flame for 2 mins.Cover the kadai with a lid and cook on low flame for 10 mins. Flip all the pieces after 10 mins and cook for another 10 mins.Now boil 3 cups of water in a large pot and add bay leaf, cinnamon stick, green cardamom, cloves, salt, ghee.When water starts boiling, add soaked & washed rice. Cook until rice is 50% done.Remove the cooked rice in a flat plate.Now add the the already prepared chicken in rice.cook it on a low flame for 15 mins.Now chicken biriyani is ready
