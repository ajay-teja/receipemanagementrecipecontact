CHICKEN CURRY RECEIPE

INGRIDENTS REQUIRED

*1 Kg chicken

*3 large onions (paste)

*2 tomatoes

*1 tbsp garlic paste

*1 tbsp ginger paste

*2 tsp dhaniya powder

*2 tsp zeera powder

*1/2 tsp haldi powder

*1/2 tsp red chilli powder

*1 tsp garam masala powder

*2 tbsp oil

*Salt

*For garnishing dhaniya leaves, chopped

PROCEDURE OF THE RECEIPE

1.Heat oil in a pan and saut√© the onion paste, garlic and ginger paste till golden brown.

2.Add tomato puree. When the tomato puree dries up, add coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, red chilli powder, turmeric powder and salt. Let the spices cook.

3.Add chicken pieces into the masala. Stir well and cook till they are golden brown.

4.Add 1/2 cup of water and stir.

5.Cover with a lid and let it simmer till the chicken is cooked.

6.Garnish with chopped coriander and serve.
