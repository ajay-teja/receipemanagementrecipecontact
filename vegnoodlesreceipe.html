VEG NOODLES RECEIPE

INGRIDENTS REQUIRED

*350 gm fresh noodles

*3 finely chopped onion

*2 sliced,chopped capsicum ( green pepper)

*3 finely chopped spring onions

*6 finely chopped green chilli

*1 teaspoon powdered white peppercorns

*1 tablespoon ajinomoto

*1/2 cup refined oil

*2 sliced,grated carrot

*100 gm sliced,grated white cabbage

*1 inch finely chopped,peeled ginger

*3 tablespoon soy sauce

*salt as required

*6 cloves minced garlic

PROCEDURE OF THE RECEIPE

Step 1:Boil the noodles

Veg Hakka Noodles are common as well as a popular recipe, which can be prepared in just a few minutes with some easily available ingredients in your kitchen. Take a large saucepan or any shallow pan and fill in some water. Then slowly leave the noodle sticks in the water and let it boil. Drain the excess water and let the noodles dry by spreading it on a plate.

Step 2:Toss the veggies

Take a deep fry pan and heat oil over medium flame. Once the oil is hot enough add onions, green chillies, ginger and garlic and saute for sometime. If you are fond of the smoky flavours, then fry the veggies on high flame. Once the veggies turn brownish, add the slightly moist noodles and stir fry.

Step 3:Cook the noodles and serve hot!

Now, add rest of the vegetables, ajinomoto, salt and pepper powder in the pan. Increase the flame and cook it for 3 to 4 minutes. Add the cooked noodles and stir well. Add soy sauce and cook for a minute more. Garnish with some spring onions and serve hot.
